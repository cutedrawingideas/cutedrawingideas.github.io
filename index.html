💡 Tips for Getting Started

📝 Start Simple

Begin with basic shapes like circles and ovals. Most cute drawings are made from simple geometric forms!

🖍️ Use Light Strokes

Start with light pencil strokes so you can easily erase mistakes. You can darken them later.

👀 Add Personality

The eyes and mouth are what make drawings cute! Experiment with different expressions.

🎨 Play with Colors

Use pastels, colored pencils, or markers to bring your drawings to life with soft, cute colors.

📚 Practice Daily

Spend just 10-15 minutes daily doodling. Consistency is key to improving your skills!

🌟 Have Fun!

Remember, there's no "perfect" drawing. Enjoy the process and let your creativity flow!
