# Cute Drawing Ideas for Website

## Categories & Specific Ideas

### 1. Animals (The Classics)
- **Elephant:** Small body, big ears, tiny trunk.
- **Sloth:** Hanging from a branch, very sleepy.
- **Cow:** Spotted patterns, simple snout.
- **Panda:** Black circles around eyes, eating bamboo.
- **Hedgehog:** Spiky back made of simple lines, tiny feet.
- **Koi Fish:** Flowing fins, simple orange/red spots.
- **Bee:** Round body, stripes, tiny wings.
- **Cat in a Planet:** A cat curled up inside a Saturn-like ring.
- **Turtle:** Round shell with simple hex patterns.

### 2. Kawaii Food & Drinks
- **Kawaii Fruits:** Watermelon slice with a face, pineapple with sunglasses.
- **Sweets:** Cupcake with a swirl, ice cream cone with a wink.
- **Japanese Drinks:** Boba tea with "smiling" pearls, Ramune bottle.
- **Breakfast:** Toast with a butter heart, egg sunny side up.

### 3. Nature & Plants
- **Cacti:** Different shapes in small pots (round, tall, flowering).
- **Wildflowers:** Simple stems with 5-petal flowers.
- **Succulents:** Layered leaf patterns in a ceramic pot.
- **Mushrooms:** Red cap with white dots (Fly Agaric style).

### 4. Seasonal & Aesthetic
- **Autumn:** Fall leaves (maple, oak), tiny pumpkins.
- **Space:** Stars, crescent moon, tiny rocket ship.
- **Cozy:** Coffee mug with steam, open book with a tiny heart.

## Design Inspiration
- **Style:** Minimalist, clean lines, pastel colors.
- **Layout:** Grid-based or masonry for ideas.
- **Interactivity:** Hover effects to show "Difficulty Level" or "Tips".
- **Sections:** Hero, Categories, Featured Idea, Footer.
